Why? Why do this?

Template for Others.

The FAARMs Project serves as an example for others to follow. Post a nominal completion, it will need continued maintenance and improvements will inevitably be made. Critical thinking is such an indispensable skill in projects like this, often, a methodology is a valuable tool. While tutorials may be created for the finer elements of this project the goal is to move makers and backyard warriors away from step-by-step handholding toward project oriented outcomes.

Educational and How To.

Indeed, tutorials and how tos have their place. Another goal of this project is to aggregate and even create materials to help non-technical types bridge the gap into a larger world. Math, science, politics, all fail to hold the interest of the average person, but when applied to applicable situations it does hold the attention of a larger proportion of the crowd.

Communal Contribution.

Finally, contributions to the now ubiquitous do-it-yourself movement with high quality contents is always valuable. Well organize projects such as this one, when considered as a whole, create the fabric of a high quality movement. By creating these systems and publishing the results, the FAARMs Project contributes to movements ranging from the localvore movement to the maker movement and all the sub niches in between.
