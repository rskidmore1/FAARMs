What is it?

The Far Away Agricultural Remote Management system or FAARMs Project endeavors to automate the monitoring, the watering, and weather watching of plants and crops in backyard gardening and small-scale farming in the DIY space. Using off-the-shelf parts and easily configurable computers, the FAARMs Project brings the power of technology to backyard agriculture.

Proof of concept.

A major part of this project is to provide proof of concept. I am not aware of any other project with the same goals and short timeline with a complementary web presence. Active projects, of this scale, with friendly documentation and an active media presence are a wholly invaluable and rare phenomena. They serve as a testament to the power of realistic expectation and follow through.

Education.

Finally, the FAARMs Project endeavors to educate inquiring minds and to advance the general state of grass-roots technologists beyond drones as toys, home Web servers, and esoteric feats of electronic craftsmanship. Technology is powerful and should be used pragmatically. What is more pragmatic than growing food? What is more basic than growing food? What is more basic than eating?
