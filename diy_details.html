DIY details

  • Monitored valve: a water valve that is open and closed by a remote server and connected to an intranet.
  • WebCams: outdoor cameras will be used to visually inspect the state of plants remotely and will be connected to an intranet, two cameras opposite each other in each section of plants to enable visual inspection from two angles.
  • Thermometer: an outdoor thermometer, connected to the intranet, will periodically take measurements throughout the day and store them.
  • Server: an on-site server will manage all the functions of the connected components. It will be connected to the Internet via a cellular data USB dongle and partially powered by a solar panel. It will be housed in the nearest enclosed structure.
  • Weather monitoring: a script will gather local weather data for the managed site, as well as other relevant data, and aggregate it into an email report.
